- Fragmented data and delayed KPI visibility.

1. Ingest and normalize source data.
2. Validate events in a canonical ledger.
3. Surface KPI performance in a command center.
4. Manage exceptions and risk hotspots.
5. Recommend optimization actions with human approval.
6. Support governance, security, and reporting readiness.
